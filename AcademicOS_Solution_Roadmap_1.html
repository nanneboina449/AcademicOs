Research Administration Module

RA-001Researcher Dashboard
As a researcher, I want to see a real-time breakdown of how I spend my time (research vs. admin) so that I can understand where my time is lost and advocate for change.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Dashboard displays time allocation pie chart (research, teaching, admin, other)
  • Admin time further broken down by activity (grant writing, reporting, compliance, meetings)
  • Comparison to department average and sector benchmarks
  • Trends over 12 months showing admin time changes
  • Export to PDF for sharing with department leadership
RA-002Grant Application Pipeline
As a researcher, I want to track all my grant applications in one place and see admin time spent at each stage so I can optimize my process.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • View all active/submitted applications with status, deadline, funding body
  • Log admin hours for each application (pre-grant, submission, post-award)
  • See average time to completion by funder (UKRI vs. Horizon Europe vs. charity)
  • Identify which stages take longest (proposal writing? Justification sections?)
  • Mobile app to log time on-the-go
RA-003Admin Bottleneck Identification
As a Research Services Director, I want to identify which administrative processes are consuming the most researcher time so we can prioritize process improvement.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Heat map showing time spent on each admin function (by stage, funder, department)
  • Identify top 5 time-consuming activities across the institution
  • Drill-down to see which departments, funder types, or application stages are problematic
  • Benchmark against other institutions (anonymized)
  • Generate report recommending which processes to redesign first

Financial Sustainability Module

FIN-0015-Year Deficit Forecast
As a Pro-Vice Chancellor for Finance, I want to forecast our financial position under multiple scenarios so I can understand the severity of our deficit and plan responses.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Model shows base case, optimistic, and pessimistic scenarios
  • Line graph showing projected cash position quarterly for 5 years
  • Identify when cash reserves are exhausted (if no action)
  • Show sensitivity analysis (which variables matter most?)
  • Export to Excel for Board presentation
FIN-002International Student Revenue Volatility
As a PVC Finance, I want to model the impact of international student enrollment changes so I can quantify our dependency risk and develop contingencies.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Current intl. student revenue by country/region with multi-year trends
  • Model scenarios: 10% recruitment drop, 20% drop, currency crisis in key market
  • See how each scenario impacts overall institutional deficit/surplus
  • Identify geographic concentration risk
  • Recommendations for geographic diversification

Student Retention & Mental Health Module

STU-001At-Risk Student Predictions
As a student support coordinator, I want to identify students at risk of dropping out before it's too late so we can intervene with support.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Dashboard shows list of students ranked by dropout risk score (0-100)
  • Risk factors broken down (academic performance, engagement, financial hardship, mental health flags)
  • Historical data: how accurate was last term's predictions?
  • See timeline of risk changes for individual students
  • Export at-risk list for action planning
STU-002Mental Health Risk Assessment
As a student support services manager, I want to identify students showing mental health risk signals so we can proactively offer support.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • System flags students based on disclosed issues (anxiety, depression, stress)
  • See cohort mental health trends (are certain cohorts struggling more?)
  • Integrate with counseling service data (students who have contacted us)
  • See wait times for first appointment and track outcome improvements
  • GDPR-compliant: sensitive data encrypted, only authorized staff access

AI Governance Module

AI-001Institutional AI Policy Assessment
As a CTO, I want to assess our current AI use and receive recommendations for institutional policy so we can govern AI responsibly.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Self-assessment questionnaire: where are we using AI?
  • Output: comparison to benchmark policies from leading institutions
  • Identify policy gaps (e.g., no assessment integrity framework)
  • Recommended policy framework tailored to our institution
  • Implementation roadmap with timelines and resource estimates
AI-002Assessment Redesign Framework
As a director of teaching and learning, I want guidance on redesigning assessments now that students use AI so we can maintain academic integrity.
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Framework recommending assessment redesign strategies
  • Identify which assessments are vulnerable to AI (essay writing) and which are resilient
  • See case studies of assessment redesign from other institutions
  • Rubric templates accounting for AI use in student work
  • Training materials for tutors on fair assessment practices
